About Us

At Mimiq we are pioneering the development of artificial intelligence by integrating the latest insights from neuroscience and machine learning.

  • Our team of scientists and engineers is driven by a shared passion for AI and its potential to improve the world. We are open-minded, curious, and collaborative, and we believe that by combining the best of neuroscience and machine learning, we can create intelligent systems that can adapt and learn in complex environments.
  • We are committed to building a diverse and inclusive team that can drive innovation and excellence in the field. We are also committed to developing and using AI in a way that is ethically responsible and aligned with our values and mission.
  • As we push the boundaries of what is possible with AI, we are excited to be at the forefront of this rapidly-evolving field. We hope you will join us on this journey as we work towards our vision of creating smart solutions that make a real difference in society and take user experiences to the next level.

Our Approach

We are building a new generation of AI, inspired by nature, that can learn and adapt to new situations.

Virtual Worlds

We use virtual worlds to train AI agents to solve complex tasks in a safe and controlled environment where they are exposed to a wide range of situations.

Temporal State

At the core of our approach is the concept of temporal state, which is an inherent property of real-time recurrent networks with processing/transport delays.

Games

We use well-known games to test new learning algorithms in limited environments with known rules and benchmarks.

Assistants

We are developing virtual assistants that can help people with their daily tasks.
